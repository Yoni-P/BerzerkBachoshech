# Project 3: 80’s

Screen resolution: 16x9

## Scope
entire game

## Intervention
Darkness - makes the game harder as the player waits for flashes of lightning to see his enemies more clearly.
Boss/BonusLevel - Not completley finished, still has some issues, but every 5 levels the player enters a special level with a machine that spawns enemies

## Mechanics
- The game map is a simple maze with 3 exits
- Player can move up down left right
- Player can shoot up down left right or diagonaly
- Enemies shoot at the player
- A special enemy "Evil Otto" shows up after a certain amount of time and follows the player
- "Evil Otto" cannot be destroyed
- The player "dies" from touching an enemy, being shot or touching a wall
- An enemy dies from being shotS
- Upon dying the player loses a life
- The player can leave a stage without destroying all enemies
- The player gets a bonus for destroying all the enemies on a level
